Gameplay Instructions

Gameplay instructions

How to play:

Every turn, the computer will "play" a series of keys and flash lights on each key. Every key has a unique sound that will also play. Every turn, you must match the keys that the computer has played.

In easy mode, the computer will generate a random sequence of 4 keys. On turn one, it will play one key. If you select the right key, it will go to round 2 and play 2 keys in sequence. Once you match the total sequence number, in this case, 4 keys played, you will win!

In phantom mode, the sequence will be random every turn.

In every mode, if you get one wrong, you restart back to round 1.

Good luck!!
